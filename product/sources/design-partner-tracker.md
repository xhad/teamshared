## Qualification rules

- 5–20 person engineering team.
- Existing multi-agent or multi-user workflow.
- Recent, costly context-loss incident.
- Willing to test real work for four weeks.
- Agrees to privacy-safe metrics and a weekly review.

Do not mark onboarding complete until agent B uses an unprompted, sourced
decision from agent A in a real task.
